Privacy Policy

Deutsch-Chinesischer Verein zur künstlichen Intelligenz e.V. ("us", "we", "our" or "GCAAI"), c/o Dr. Kashif Rasul, Chausseestr. 37 D 2, 10115 Berlin, operates the https://gcaai.org website (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data, in accordance with Article 13 of the EU Data Protection Basic Regulation (DS-GVO).

Person responsible for data processing and contact details of data protection officers

According to Art. 4 para. 7 EU Data Protection Basic Regulation (DS-GVO), the responsible person is the Deutsch-Chinesischer Verein zur künstlichen Intelligenz e.V., c/o Dr. Kashif Rasul, Chausseestr. 37 D 2, 10115 Berlin, contact@gcaai.org.

Our data protection officer is at your disposal at contact@gcaai.org or at our postal address with the addition "Attn. the data protection officer" for queries regarding our data protection.

Purposes of processing and categories of personal data

We process personal data pursuant to Art. 4 DS-GVO and special personal data (e.g. interests) pursuant to Article 9 (1) DS-GVO in accordance with the provisions of the EU Data Protection Basic Regulation (DSGVO) and the Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG-neu):

We process special personal data pursuant to Art. 9 para. 2 d) DS-GVO on the basis of suitable guarantees within the scope of our lawful activities and pursuant to Art. 9 para. 2 a) or Art. 6 DS-GVO on the basis of your consent.

If you have given us permission to process personal data for certain purposes (e.g. passing on data to our associations, processing enquiries, invitations to information events), the legality of this processing is given on the basis of your consent.

A given consent can be revoked at any time. Please note that the revocation will only take effect in the future. Processing that took place before the revocation is not affected by this.

Recipient of personal data

Within the GCAAI, the ones who will have access to your information are those who need to respond to your request and/or to comply with the membership and statutory requirements of obligation contract processors commissioned by us. They can also receive data for these purposes if they guarantee the requirements of the EU Data Protection Basic Regulation / the Federal Data Protection Act.

Data Storage

We store the data collected in Amazon AWS S3 and google Drive in an encrypted. We will keep your registration data with the duration you being a member of GCAAI. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by erasing them on both AWS S3 and Google Drive.

Usage of Cookies

We don't use cookies to remember user information on our services.

Transfer of data to a third country

Since we use Amazon AWS and google drive to process and store the data. A data transfer to countries outside the EU or the EEA (so-called third countries) takes place.

Your information, including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from your jurisdiction.

If you are located outside Germany and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data, including Personal Data, to Germany and process it there.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

Deutsch-Chinesischer Verein zur künstlichen Intelligenz e.V. will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of your Personal Data will take place to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of your data and other personal information.

Duration of processing

We only process your data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the above-mentioned purposes or applicable legal regulations.

Business documents, contribution payments or donation payments are kept in accordance with the legal requirements.

Should you wish your data to be deleted, we will delete your data immediately unless legal retention periods prevent the deletion.

Automated decision making or profiling

We do not perform automated decision making or profiling.

  • Revocation of your given consent with effect for the future.

    Mir ist bekannt, dass der Antrag auf Mitgliedschaft ohne diese Zustimmung nicht gewährt werden kann.
